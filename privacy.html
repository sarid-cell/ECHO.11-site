Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

At ECHO.11, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and use on this website.

Information We Collect

This website is primarily informational and does not require user registration or login. We may collect limited information in the following ways:

  • Analytics Data: We use Google Analytics (Google's gtag.js) to understand how visitors interact with our site. This may include anonymized data such as page views, referring sites, device type, and general geographic location. Google may set cookies to enable this. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.
  • Payment Information: If you purchase a digital product, payment is processed entirely by PayPal. We do not receive or store your card or PayPal account details — only confirmation that a purchase was completed. See PayPal's Privacy Policy for details.
  • Contact Information: If you choose to contact us via email, we will receive and store the information you provide, including your email address and message content.
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies for basic website functionality, and analytics cookies set by Google Analytics as described above. We do not use cookies for third-party advertising.

How We Use Information

Any information collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To improve the website's content and user experience
  • To respond to inquiries and communications
  • To analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • To process and confirm digital product purchases via PayPal

Third-Party Services

This website uses the following third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy: Google Analytics (website usage analytics) and PayPal (payment processing for digital products). The site may also link to external sites, including Substack for articles and social media platforms. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external services.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect any information collected through this website. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access information we hold about you
  • The right to request correction or deletion of your data
  • The right to opt out of certain data collection practices

International Users

This website is operated from Israel. If you are accessing this site from the European Union, United States, or other regions with data protection laws, please be aware that your information may be transferred to and processed in Israel.

Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
